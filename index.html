How it works

SkillSync is built to be fast, simple, and useful while you browse job postings.

1

Set up your resume once

Upload or paste your resume a single time so SkillSync has a profile to compare against future job postings.

2

Analyze the job posting in-browser

The extension detects the current job post on supported boards and company career pages, then extracts skills, tools, qualifications, and responsibilities.

3

Read the report in 30 seconds

Get a compact side-panel report with matched skills, missing skills, importance tags, and actionable recommendations for what to learn, add, or showcase.
