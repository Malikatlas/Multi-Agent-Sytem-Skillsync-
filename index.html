How it works

Designed for speed: open a job post, upload your resume once, and get a clear fit summary without leaving the browser.

1

Detect the job posting

SkillSync reads the active browser tab and extracts the job title, company, required skills, preferred skills, responsibilities, and key qualifications.

2

Parse your resume

Upload your resume once per session so SkillSync can identify your skills, tools, experience signals, and domain keywords for comparison.

3

Compare and recommend

See matched skills, missing skills, partial matches, and the most important next skill to learn based on the posting itself.
