How it works

SkillSync is designed to fit naturally into your job search flow: open a posting, scan your resume, and instantly get a decision-ready comparison.

1

Open a job posting in your browser

SkillSync parses the page and extracts the main required, preferred, and repeated skills, tools, and qualifications directly from the listing.

2

Upload or paste your resume

Bring in a resume file or pasted text. The extension compares your background to the role without asking you to reformat anything.

3

Get a concise skills-gap report

See matched, partially matched, and missing skills, plus the top 3 priorities to address before applying.

4

Follow specific next-step recommendations

Receive practical guidance such as what to emphasize, which projects to add, and which learning focus will close the most important gaps.

Target users

SkillSync is useful across experience levels, whether users are building a first portfolio, changing careers, or evaluating advanced roles.

Fresh Graduate Data Job Seeker

A recent graduate applying to entry-level data analyst, data scientist, and software roles with limited professional experience and a project-heavy resume.

  • Cannot tell which job requirements are essential versus optional
  • Needs guidance on what to learn or highlight before applying

Career Pivoter into Data Science

A professional transitioning from another field into data science who has partial technical skills and wants to know how close they are to role requirements.

  • Has difficulty mapping past experience to job requirements
  • Needs specific, prioritized gaps to focus learning effort efficiently

Mid-level ML Engineer

An experienced machine learning engineer targeting senior-level roles and seeking a fast way to assess readiness against more advanced postings.

  • Wants to quickly identify missing senior-level skills like leadership, deployment, or system design
  • Needs a concise comparison that respects existing expertise without generic advice

Why SkillSync matters

Job seekers lose time guessing. SkillSync replaces uncertainty with a focused, role-specific comparison that makes each application more intentional.

Instead of reading a posting and hoping your resume is a fit, you can quickly see what matters most and where you stand.
