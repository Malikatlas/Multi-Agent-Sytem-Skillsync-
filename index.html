How it works

A fast workflow built for people comparing roles while browsing job boards.

1

Upload your resume once

Drop in a PDF or DOCX, or paste your resume text. SkillSync extracts skills, roles, tools, and experience signals into a reusable profile.

2

Analyze the active job posting

The browser extension reads the posting you're viewing and identifies required skills, preferred skills, responsibilities, and seniority cues.

3

Get your gap report instantly

SkillSync compares both sides and returns matched skills, missing skills, and prioritized recommendations with concrete next actions.

Core features

Everything in the MVP is focused on tech and data roles, with speed and clarity as the priority.

Resume Upload and Parsing

Upload a resume PDF/DOCX or paste text to extract structured skills, roles, tools, and experience signals for reuse across applications.

Job Posting Skill Extraction

The extension reads the active job posting and extracts required and preferred skills, responsibilities, and seniority cues.

Skills Gap Analysis

Compare your resume against the posting to identify matched skills, missing skills, and partially evidenced skills.

Personalized Gap Report

See a concise report with missing skills, importance signals from the posting, and a ranked priority list.

Concrete Next-Step Recommendations

Get one relevant course, one project idea, and one resume bullet improvement for each major gap so you can act immediately.

Saved Profile for Reuse

Store your parsed profile and compare against multiple postings without re-uploading every time.

Why SkillSync matters

Most job seekers lose time on vague advice. SkillSync turns a posting into a practical action plan.

The problem

Tech and data job seekers often cannot quickly tell which required skills they are missing, which ones matter most, and what to do next. That uncertainty slows applications and makes resume tailoring feel guesswork-heavy.

The solution

SkillSync provides a browser-extension MVP that analyzes the current job posting, compares it to your resume, and returns a personalized skills-gap report with concrete recommendations within 30 seconds.
