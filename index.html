SkillSync Browser Extension

Seamlessly while you browse LinkedIn, Indeed, and Glassdoor, SkillSync detects job postings, compares required skills with your uploaded resume, and provides actionable skills-gap reports in real time.

Key Features

Job Posting Capture

Monitors your browsing on LinkedIn, Indeed, and Glassdoor to detect and extract job posting details, including the required skills, automatically.

Resume Upload & Parsing

Upload your resume to SkillSync to extract your existing skills for accurate comparison with job listings and identify gaps.

Skills Extraction & Comparison

Analyzes and compares skills required by the job against those on your resume, and highlights the missing or underrepresented skills you need to develop.

Personalized Skills-Gap Report

Gets you a clear, customized report showing which skills you lack, ranked by urgency and relevance for each specific job role you view.

Upskilling Recommendations

Suggests courses, certifications, and resources specifically tailored for your missing skills, complete with links and estimated learning effort.

Privacy and Data Security

Your resume and browsing activity are handled securely with transparent controls — your data stays private and protected at all times.
