How it works

SkillSync keeps the workflow fast and focused: open a job post, import your resume, and get a specific comparison that helps you decide whether to apply.

1

Detect the job post

The extension recognizes supported career pages and extracts the role title, company, responsibilities, required skills, preferred skills, and seniority signals from the page.

2

Parse your resume

Upload a PDF or DOCX and SkillSync structures your resume into a skills profile, including tools, technologies, keywords, and experience signals.

3

Compare and decide

SkillSync identifies matched, missing, and partially matched skills, then returns a clear readiness summary with targeted next steps tailored to that exact job posting.

Built for speed, clarity, and job-specific relevance — without turning into a generic career tool.

🧠

Job post scraping and skill extraction

Resume import and parsing
