Everything you need to turn job descriptions into action.

Built for early-career job seekers, career switchers, and active job hunters who need fast, repeatable feedback without manually decoding every posting.

1

Job-post detection across supported sites

Recognizes job-detail pages on LinkedIn, Indeed, and Glassdoor, including modals and dynamic SPA updates.

2

Extraction and normalization

Pulls out skills, certifications, tools, seniority, responsibilities, and salary signals, then normalizes synonyms into a canonical taxonomy.

3

Resume parsing and matching

Uploads PDF, DOCX, or plain text resumes, builds a skill profile, and compares it against each posting automatically.

4

Skills-gap scoring

Separates must-have, preferred, and adjacent skills so you know what matters most before you apply.

5

Actionable gap report

Shows missing skills, the exact evidence that triggered each gap, plus courses, projects, certifications, and resume wording ideas.

6

Privacy and consent controls

Requires opt-in, supports pausing by site, and provides deletion controls for resumes, reports, and extracted job data.

How it works

A simple workflow that helps you go from browsing to a clear next step in seconds.

1

Install and opt in

Add the extension, grant permission for supported job sites, and upload your resume once.

2

Open a job posting

SkillSync detects job-detail views automatically and extracts the role’s requirements and skills.

3

Get your gap report

Review your fit score, missing skills by priority, evidence from the post, and specific ways to close the gaps.
